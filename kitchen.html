Exercise


After scuttling around the interior of the house for a bit, you've managed to make you way to the kitchen. MARVELOUS. The variety of different smells invade your nostrils and you become overwhelmed.
Your owner seems to be following right behind you! You run straight towards his ankles in an attempt to get him to notice your tiny being. He picks you up, takes you out of your rolly orb and places you in a basket of crackers.
You contemplate your original goal but get distracted by the sweet melody of the crackers calling to you.
The rest of you day is spent in bliss with the crackers, but you didn't complete any goals.
