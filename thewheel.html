EXERCISE


After several minutes of struggling you manage to get free from your binds. Its time... TO WORK OFF THAT PUDGE.
You waddle over to the wheel and catch sight of Lulu (the hammy you've had a crush on for quite some time now), suddenly your motivation to exercise multiplied tenfold. Working off your pudge will mean that you'll have more of a chance with Lulu.
Immediately you begin sprinting on the wheel. This is it. This is how you'll win her heart, but the speed of the wheel increased faster than you expected. NOOOO. Hammy hearts are too weak for this much excitement at once!!!
You're sent spiraling to your doom.

YOU LOSE.
