Exercise


Ahh, it's finally the weekend (not that the difference between a weekend or a weekday mattered to a hamster anyways but..) There's a fresh bowl and seeds out for your morning meal and you're just about to indulge yourself in them when...
*doink*
You can't... Seem to get out of your nest??? A wave a panic rushes over you.
Oh no! What's happening? Are your stubby legs now paralyzed? Do you even HAVE stubby legs anymore? WHAT DO.
Wait a sec... Oh. Your circumference has enlarged to encompass the entirety of you nest.
So in other words you've managed to get so pudgey that you fit a little too snuggly into your nest.
THE WHEEL
I mean... I guess that means you should get on the wheel and exercise! Wiggle yourself out of your nest and head on over there!
STUCK
Hmmm... No matter how hard you wiggle your lil hammy buns, you can't manage to get out of that nest ):
Welp. You decide to sit there until you owner comes by and puts you into that rolly orb.
